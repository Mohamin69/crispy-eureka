About HAMZA Beauty

HAMZA Beauty curates affordable, everyday personal care products selected for gentle performance and broad skin compatibility. We source trusted brands and stock items locally to reduce wait times for customers in Tamale and surrounding areas.

Our Promise

We prioritise products with balanced formulations — effective hydration, non-irritating fragrances, and clear ingredient labeling. If you have sensitive skin, check the ingredients list and perform a patch test.

Sourcing & Quality

Products are sourced from established distributors and inspected on arrival. Where available we provide ingredient and usage guidance to help you choose the right item for your skin type.

How to choose and use

Identify your skin needs

Look for 'hydrating' if you feel tightness, or 'non-comedogenic' if you are prone to breakouts.

Follow directions

Apply lotions on damp skin, creams to dry patches at night, and sprays from a short distance onto clothing or pulse points.

Patch test new items

Apply a small amount to the inner forearm for 24 hours to check for reactions before wide use.

Ingredients & Safety

We list common active ingredients and explanations so you can make informed choices. If you have a known allergy, always check ingredient lists carefully.

  • Glycerin: Humectant that attracts moisture to the skin.
  • Emollients: Soften and smooth the skin surface; used in creams and lotions.
  • Alcohol denat.: Common in sprays and may be drying; avoid on broken skin.
  • Fragrance: Adds scent but may irritate sensitive skin; fragrance-free options are available.
